Generative AI for Work & Research

An 8-week, mentor-supported bootcamp (University of Geneva-affiliated GRAPH Courses) focused on no-code AI tools for task automation and practical research workflows.

  • Weekly live workshops & help sessions; private community forums
  • Personalized feedback, capstone coaching, and shareable certificate
  • Lifetime access to the tutorial library

Modules (highlights)

  • 1. LLMs 101; Markdown/LaTeX/HTML; building simple research sites
  • 2. AI-automated data analysis; dashboards; batch file workflows
  • 3. GenAI for research & text analysis; literature review tools
  • 4. Custom GPTs & chatbots; RAG and knowledge bases
  • 5. AI for task automation; agentic browser workflows
  • 6. Communication & outreach; figures, videos, prompts
  • 7. Capstone project & showcase

Grading rubric

  • Online quizzes — 15%
  • Workshop assignments — 35%
  • Attendance & participation — 10%
  • Final project — 40% (pass ≥ 80%; attend ≥ 5 workshops)

Instructors/mentors include faculty and researchers from University of Geneva and partners.
